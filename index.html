What is AURA?

Aura is an internationally recognized and award winning virtual technical fest conducted by Women In Engineering Affinity Group, Women In Power and Women In Computing of IEEE SCT SB, exclusively for women and in this second edition, we have a plethora of competitions, webinars and talks by eminent personalities, from November 27 to December 8, 2021.
