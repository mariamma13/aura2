Do you have Questions?

Why Aura?

The idea behind Aura is to provide a platform for the female students to come forward, to compete with each other and to uncover their hidden potential.

What can you expect from AURA 2.0?

Aura 2.0 is one of the significant events of our SB. In this edition, we have planned some exciting, fun-filled and insightful events which consists of competitions, workshops, interactive talk sessions and a cultural night. It will be a great opportunity for all the women to come out of their comfort zone and build their skills and confidence.

What is the maximum number of competitions a participant can take part in?

The participants can take part in any number of competitions depending on their convenience.

What is the maximum participation count per competition from a Student Branch?

There is no limit to maximum count of participation from an SB. But only a maximum of 15 participation points per competition are awarded for each SB. The winner points (if any) will be added to this 15.

Can the SB representatives participate in the competitions?

Any female student is welcome to compete.

Is home participation allowed?

Home participation is allowed but while considering the final results of the competitions, the points acquired by our college students will not be considered. If there are any home winners, two lists of winners will be announced. One including the home winners and the other excluding them.
